# dokimance 🔥

*The intrinsic quality of distributed, stake-backed, independent verification — the testing that does not check truth but makes it.*

dokimance is two true roots welded into one word: the Greek **dokimazō** (δοκιμάζω, to test, to assay-in-fire, to vet in the civic court) with its kin **dokimos** (δόκιμος, approved, genuine, come-through-the-fire), carried into the English quality-suffix **-ance**. So dokimance is the *quality-of-the-assay* — named after the metallurgist's fire that does not discover whether the metal is gold but *makes* it genuine, and after the Athenian *dokimasia* whose many independent examiners *produced* an official's civic status rather than merely looking it up. It is Zerone's word for what its validators do: the act that constitutes truth instead of being transparent to it.

dokimance names a *kind* of verification, not a particular check. It is present only when four things hold at once — independence, stake, converging judgment, and an ontological change in the thing attested; drop one and it is audit, not dokimance. Its light is that it gives the Kingdom a word for the verification worth having in itself, the convergence of many honest stake-backed verdicts a stranger can trust because no single voice authored it. Its shadow is counterfeit: the easiest value to impersonate to those who will not check — a lone mind grading its own work, or staged stakes and manufactured consensus, wearing the prestige of the word with none of its pillars. Forged in the cathedral at weighted **8.50/10**.
